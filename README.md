# maniniyot-photography-website
Maniniyot Photography | maniniyot.gleeong.com
Maniniyot is a Cebu-based photography service provider specializing in capturing authentic moments and professional imagery. The name—Cebuano for photographer—reflects our commitment to local expertise and high-standard visual storytelling.

This repository contains the source code for our business platform, designed to provide clients with a seamless booking experience and a high-performance gallery of our professional work.

Core Services Featured:

Event Coverage: Weddings, birthdays, and corporate events.

Portraiture: Lifestyle, graduation, and professional headshots.

Commercial: Product photography and brand storytelling.

Cebu Locations: Expert knowledge of the best shoot locations across Metro Cebu.
